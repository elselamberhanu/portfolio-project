Favourite book

My recent read favourite book is Blood Over Bright Haven by M.L. Wang. It is a fantasy novel set in a world where a specific privilged "chosen" group has access to magic and saftey of a barrier that protectets them from a supposed curse they call Blight while the "cursed" ones are doomed to live in terror, suffeing and forced to live in oppression. It deals with heavy topics such as religous fanaticism, false history, misogeny, abuse, oppression, power and good and evil. I gives us an indepth insight in to the working of government and society that leaves you contemplating with a personal favourite perfect ending.
